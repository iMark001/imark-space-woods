FK Plywood — Grades and Applications

Grade is determined by surface quality: first digit — face, back — back. The lower the number, the better the finish. Each grade covers a specific market..

I / I Premium
Both sides — top grade, surface without visible defects. Furniture fronts, decorative panels, visible interior finishes.
I / II
Face — defect-free, back — minor knots permitted. Furniture, retail equipment, display structures.
II / II
Both sides first grade with minimal permitted defects. Interior panels, wall cladding, floors for finishing.
II / III Popular
Optimal price-to-quality ratio. Flooring, drawers, shelving, cladding — the main construction and furniture segment.
III / IV
Structural grade. Formwork for monolithic construction, temporary floors, rough works. High load-bearing capacity.
IV / IV Technical
Maximum surface defect — technical grade. Pallets, packaging, construction underlays. High volume at minimum price.
SHOP Sanded
Surface sanded on one or both sides. Ready for finishing — paint, varnish, veneer, laminate. Furniture production, flooring.
II / IV
One side clean, the other with permitted defects. Construction work where only one surface quality matters.
III / III
Both sides structural grade. Through knots, repaired defects. Formwork, temporary structures, non-residential construction.
